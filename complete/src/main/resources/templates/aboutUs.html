Our Mission

Quizmaster is an web-app, made for improving acess to quality education throughout the world.

Through QuizMaster, we hope to provide teachers a platfrom to host lessons and quizzes for their respective students to complete. This enables remote access for learning and helps continue education through unforseen circumstances.
